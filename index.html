Legal support & compliance

Legal Support, Compliance & Lawyer Ran Consultancy

We are a legal support, compliance and lawyer-run consultancy for England and Wales. We help you understand your position in plain‑English, prepare practical documents and run structured dispute support on unreserved matters, so you can respond proportionately and build robust pre‑claim material. For comparable early‑stage work, we are typically cheaper than instructing a traditional solicitor for that kind of work, which helps you manage expense before you decide whether to pursue a formal claim.

Our information and documents are framed for the law of England and Wales only. We are not a law firm; we do not advise on Scots law, Northern Irish law or foreign jurisdictions, and we are not regulated by the SRA.

Where you need reserved work or court representation, we will recommend a regulated solicitor and can still help in the background on facts, chronology and drafts.
