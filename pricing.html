Why use Resolutor first

A more affordable first move than going straight to a solicitor

For early-stage, unreserved dispute work, using us is usually cheaper than instructing a traditional solicitor to do comparable correspondence and preparation. We help put your position into a clear, authoritative letter with the facts, deadline and requested outcome set out properly.

Where appropriate, Resolutor can send correspondence on your behalf as an independent legal support service. A firm, well-structured letter from us can make the issue feel more serious, harder to ignore and more likely to be paid or resolved before costs escalate.

If the matter sits outside our scope or needs representation, we work with a variety of solicitors and can help you find suitable options and compare pricing before you commit.

Cost control

Why early structure matters

Disputes tend to become more expensive as positions harden, papers multiply and urgent decisions replace measured ones. Our work is designed to create clarity before that curve steepens.

What affects the fee

The variables we price around

  • Volume How many documents, messages, invoices, photographs or witness accounts need reviewing.
  • Urgency Whether the matter can be handled steadily or needs a fast turnaround.
  • Complexity Number of parties, disputed facts, specialist context and whether reputation or family issues are involved.
  • Output Whether you need one letter, a full complaint pack, ongoing correspondence or a monthly service.

Service scope

What the work can include

Letters before action

Clear, firm letters that set out the facts, the outcome sought and a proportionate timetable. Where appropriate, we can send the letter on your behalf so the other side receives structured, authoritative correspondence from Resolutor.

Complaints handling

Policies, templates and proportionate responses for operators of all sizes, plus support for individuals bringing or answering complaints.

Mediation preparation

Issue lists, chronologies, settlement framing and practical preparation so negotiations are focused rather than reactive.

Solicitor introductions

We are not a law firm and do not provide reserved legal services. If a matter is outside our speciality, needs court representation or requires litigation conduct, we can help source suitable solicitor options and compare fees before you choose who to instruct.
