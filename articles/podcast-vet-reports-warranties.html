Bloodstock and sales

Podcast: vet reports, warranties and cooling off—what buyers and sellers miss

12 March 2026

In this episode we walk through where sales disputes actually start: mismatched expectations about vetting, what a five‑stage report does and does not prove, and how cooling‑off or consumer rules interact with private and trade sales in practice.

We are not offering a substitute for regulated advice on a specific transaction. The aim is to help buyers and sellers ask better questions before money moves—and to recognise when paperwork or allegations have crossed into territory where solicitors and experts need to be in the room.
